Campain Poster for ACT.IL organization

During the Graphic Design course there was a competition to create a poster for the ACT.IL campain. My poster got chosen (10 posters out of 70)to represent the campain for Israels 70 year celebration. I chose to focus on the diversity we have in Israel and how we should ideally cope with it.

2018 New Year Card

In our graphic design course we had a task to make 2018 Happy new year cards. I chose to represent the year with a chick popping out of its shell to represent a new start.
